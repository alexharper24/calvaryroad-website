Evangelism & Soul Winning

"Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature." (Mark 16:15)

Our Heart for the Lost

The command to give the gospel to the world is clear and unmistakable, and it was given to the church. We take it seriously. Reaching lost people with the good news of Jesus Christ is at the very center of who we are as a congregation.

"How then shall they call on him in whom they have not believed? And how shall they believe in him of whom they have not heard? And how shall they hear without a preacher?" (Romans 10:14). Every week our people go out into New Albany and the surrounding communities to share Christ, invite families to church, and see souls saved.

How We Reach Our Community

Saturday Visitation

Every Saturday at 10:30 AM we go out door to door to share the gospel, encourage church families, and invite people to come and hear the Word.

Bus & Van Routes

Two bus routes and a van route reach children and families across the area and bring them to church each week.

Faith Promise Missions

We send the gospel far beyond our own community by supporting missionaries at home and around the world.

Revivals & Friend Days

Special meetings, revivals, and friend days give our people a natural way to invite neighbors, coworkers, and friends to hear the gospel.

Community Outreach

From festivals to a soul-winning booth at the Kentucky State Fair, we look for every opportunity to reach people where they are.

Personal Soul Winning

Evangelism is not just for the pulpit. We equip every believer to share his or her faith with confidence and compassion.

Learn to Share the Gospel

Are you a believer who wants to learn how to lead someone to Christ? This message from evangelist Randy O'Brien walks through how to share the gospel simply and clearly from the Bible.

Come Soul Winning With Us

Whether you have been witnessing for years or have never done it before, you are welcome to join us. Meet us at the church on Saturday at 10:30 AM, or call and we will help you get started.
