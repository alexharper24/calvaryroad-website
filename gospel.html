God's Simple Plan

How can I know for sure that I am going to Heaven? Let's see what the Bible says about it.

Am I good enough on my own?

None of Us Is Righteous

Many people believe they are good enough to earn Heaven. But God's Word tells us that, measured against His perfect standard, not one of us is good enough on our own.

Romans 3:10-12

The Problem

We Are All Sinners

Sin is falling short of God's perfect glory. Every one of us has done it, and it separates us from a holy God.

Romans 3:23

The Penalty

Sin Earns Death

There is a price for sin, and that price is death, eternal separation from God. But in the same breath, God offers a gift we could never earn.

Romans 6:23

The Payment

God's Love Made a Way

We cannot save ourselves by good works. God's love made a way through His Son. While we were still sinners, Christ died for us, and He is the only way to the Father.

Romans 5:8 Ephesians 2:8-9 John 14:6

The Decision

Trust Christ and Be Saved

To be saved you must confess your sins and ask Jesus to come into your heart and save you. Salvation is a free gift, received by faith. Whosoever will may come.

Romans 10:9 Romans 10:13
